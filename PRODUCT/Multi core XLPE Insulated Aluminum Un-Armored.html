    Technical Data

    • Reference: IS 7098 PT-I.
    • Voltage Grade: Up to and including 1100 volt.
    • Conductor: EC grade Annealed Aluminum class 1 & 2 as per IS 8130.
    • Insulation: PVC Type – A, as per 5831.
    • Colour of Core: Red & black for 2 core cable, red, yellow, blue for 3 core cable, red, yellow, blue & black for 4 core cable.
    • Sheath: PVC Type ST-I, PVC Type -ST 2 as per IS 5831.
    • Colour of Sheath: Black and Other Colour as per Customer demand.
    • Marking & Packing: Standard packing of 500 mtr. in coil. & Other length available on request, cables are printed with marking of ‘ANOCAB’.
